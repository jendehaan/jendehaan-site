Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What information I collect

I collect information in a few different ways to help me communicate with you and understand how you're using this site.

When you sign up for the newsletter, I collect your first name and email address through Kit.com. When you use the contact form, I collect your name, email address, and message through Formspree. I also collect basic analytics data about how you use this site through Google Analytics, such as which pages you visit and how long you spend on them.

How I use your information

I use your information to send you the newsletter you've signed up for, to respond to messages you send me through the contact form, and to understand how people are using this site so I can improve it. I do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

This site uses three main third-party services that may collect or process your information:

  • Kit.com — Handles newsletter signups and email delivery. They store your first name and email address.
  • Formspree — Handles contact form submissions. They store the information you submit through the contact form.
  • Google Analytics — Collects analytics data about your use of this site, including pages visited, time on site, and device information.
  • GitHub Pages — Hosts this website. GitHub may collect basic server logs.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy, and your data is processed according to their terms. I encourage you to review their privacy policies directly.

Cookies & Tracking

This site uses cookies to provide a better user experience and to analyze traffic via Google Analytics. Kit.com may also use cookies for newsletter management purposes. When you first visit the site, you are presented with a cookie consent banner allowing you to acknowledge our use of cookies. You can also control and restrict cookie settings directly within your web browser preferences.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email I send. If you want to request deletion of your data, you can contact me using the contact form and I'll help you with that request within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your information is being used, please reach out through the contact page.
